ABOUT ME

Me & Design

I am a junior at Brown University, studying Computer Science. My main two pathways are Artificial Intelligence and Visual Computing. Although my two pathways are not related to UIUX, I am planning on doing a fifth-years masters in the Design pathway; that is what prompted me to want to take CSCI1300 this fall semester. CSCI1300 is the first design-related class that I've ever taken, and it is the first class that I was introduced to front-end development. I was able to learn a lot about new programming languages: html, css, javascript, react, and more! There is a bit of learning curve to these languages. Although I still do not have a comprehensive understanding for all these languages, I was able to complete all assigments and finish off the semester with this portfolio!
